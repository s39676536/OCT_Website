AI Diagnosis Platform For AMD

Age-related macular degeneration (AMD) is one of the causes of vision impairment in older adult and there is no permanent cure for AMD. Early detection and successive treatment may slow down the disease progression and reserve the vision. Optical coherence tomography (OCT) was one of the best way to diagnose AMD. However, the number of patients continue to increase and manual OCT assessment in clinical practice has become unrealistic. Hence, we developed Artificial intelligence (AI) to deal with this problem. With only OCT images, AI automatically identify four types of AMD, normal macular, drusen, inactive wet AMD, and active wet AMD. Furthermore, we established a website for realistic cloud service according to the AI platform. The address of our website is available at https://www.ym.edu.tw/~AI-OCT/. Our AI technology with enormous potential for applying to many medical territory such as orthopedics, gastroenterology and oncology. Besides, the AI platform can be integrated into telemedicine and remote care and we wish ameliorate the medical services in remote areas and offshore islands.
